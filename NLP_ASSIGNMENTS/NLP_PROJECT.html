// Features

Everything you need to
never miss a bus

📡
Auto-Detection via Geofencing
The moment a bus enters your 500m radius, you're notified automatically. No manual searching — the system detects buses near you in real time.
🗺️
Live GPS Map
Watch every campus bus move in real time on an interactive map. See exact positions, routes, and next stops updated every 3 seconds.
⏱️
Smart ETA Predictions
AI-powered arrival time predictions that account for traffic, past patterns, and campus events. ETAs accurate to within 30 seconds.
🔔
Push Notifications
Get alerts when your bus is 2 minutes away, when it's delayed, or when a new bus is added to your route. Fully customizable.
📶
Offline Mode
Even with spotty campus Wi-Fi, cached schedules and last-known positions keep you informed. Syncs instantly when connection is restored.
🧭
Route Planner
Enter your destination and get the best bus combination to reach it, with walking directions to the nearest stop and real-time connections.
// How It Works

Four steps to
smart tracking

01
Location Shared
You share your location once. The system continuously watches for buses entering your radius.
02
Buses Detected
GPS trackers on every campus bus report position every 3 seconds to our central server.
03
ETA Calculated
ML models compute arrival time factoring in route, traffic, stops remaining, and campus events.
04
You're Notified
Instant alerts sent via push notification, in-app, and optionally via WhatsApp or email.
